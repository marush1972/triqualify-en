About TriQualify
TriQualify is an independent qualification calculator for age group triathletes targeting the IRONMAN World Championship in Kona, the IRONMAN 70.3 World Championship, or the XTERRA World Championship.

All results are estimates based on historical race data from 2025. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by IRONMAN, the World Triathlon Corporation, or XTERRA.
